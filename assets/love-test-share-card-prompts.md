# Love-Test Share Card Prompts

## Visual Direction

Create premium Tianji Love share cards for a private love-pattern result. Keep the tone intimate, elegant, and useful. Do not show birth date, birth time, birth location, timezone, private questions, or raw result payloads.

## OG Card

Wide cinematic card with a deep night background, warm gold typography, subtle astrological line work, and a large result score. Include the archetype, one-line insight, and the call to action: "Take the free Love Test".

## WeChat Moments

Square card optimized for mobile social feeds. Use a clear central score, a short headline, three keyword chips, and a footer that says: "Private result. Birth data is not collected."

## Xiaohongshu

Editorial portrait card with a polished lifestyle feel, soft contrast, gold dividers, and a strong title. Keep the layout readable at small sizes and avoid dense explanatory copy.

## Douyin

Vertical short-video cover style. Use high contrast, large score, punchy headline, and a clear bottom CTA: "Ask one relationship question next".
