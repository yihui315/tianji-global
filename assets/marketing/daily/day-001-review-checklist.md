### Douyin

- Keep scripts under 25 seconds for Day 1.
- Use captions that frame Love Test as reflection, not prediction.
- Do not imply the creator knows what the other person thinks.

### Videohao

- Keep tone slower and more mature.
- Emphasize privacy, boundaries, and self-reflection.
- Avoid urgency-driven paid language.

### KOL outreach

- Ask for honest feedback, not endorsement.
- Do not request fake testimonials.
- Do not imply payment results or user numbers.
- Ask permission before any public case reference.
