# TianJi Voice & Tone Guide

> This document defines how TianJi speaks — in UI copy, marketing pages, reports, disclaimers, and AI-generated summaries. Every team member and every AI prompt should follow these guidelines.

---

## 1. Brand Voice

TianJi's voice sits at the intersection of four qualities:

| Quality | What it means | What it does NOT mean |
|---|---|---|
| **Mystical** | Evokes wonder, depth, and the vastness of classical systems | "Spooky", superstitious, or vague |
| **Premium** | Confident, polished, world-class presentation | Cold, arrogant, or inaccessible |
| **Reassuring** | Warm, supportive, empowering | Cheap self-help slogans or toxic positivity |
| **Professional** | Precise, evidence-referenced, methodologically honest | Dry academic jargon or clinical detachment |

**One-liner:** *"We speak like a knowledgeable guide who respects the user's intelligence and emotional state."*

---

## 2. Do / Don't

### ✅ Do

- Use concrete outcomes: *"3-minute dual-engine reading"*
- Reference methodology: *"Swiss Ephemeris precision", "classical Zi Wei algorithm"*
- Empower the user: *"Your chart suggests…", "The structure leans toward…"*
- Be specific about what each feature delivers
- Use one language at a time — switch cleanly, never mix mid-sentence

### ❌ Don't

- Make absolute predictions: ~~"You WILL get rich this year"~~
- Use empty mystical filler: ~~"The universe has a message for you"~~
- Repeat the same idea in both languages on the same line: ~~"开始你的命运探索 · Begin Your Journey"~~
- Use fear-based urgency: ~~"Your fate is at risk — act now!"~~
- Over-promise AI capability: ~~"Our AI knows your future"~~

---

## 3. Hero Copy Rules

| Rule | Example |
|---|---|
| Lead with structure, not prophecy | ✅ "Understand yourself through your chart" · ❌ "We predict your future" |
| Keep Chinese ≤ 12 characters per headline | ✅ "从命盘结构理解自己" |
| English headlines: 6 words max | ✅ "Your chart, decoded" |
| One subtitle, one language | Don't stack ZH + EN subtitles |
| Include a method anchor | "Swiss Ephemeris precision · classical algorithms" |

---

## 4. CTA Copy Rules

| Rule | Example |
|---|---|
| Action-first, benefit-second | ✅ "Begin Your Journey" · ❌ "Click here" |
| Quantify when possible | ✅ "3-minute dual-engine reading" |
| Avoid vague verbs | ❌ "Discover", "Unlock", "Unleash" without a concrete object |
| Keep CTA ≤ 5 words (EN) or ≤ 8 characters (ZH) | ✅ "开始探索" · ❌ "立即开启你的神秘命运探索之旅" |

---

## 5. Pricing Copy Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Name plans with brand identity | "Explore", "Stellar", "TianJi Pro" — not "Basic / Pro / Enterprise" |
| Tagline = what the user gets | ✅ "Full charts + AI deep analysis" · ❌ "Our best plan" |
| List features as outcomes | ✅ "All 12 divination tools" · ❌ "Premium access" |
| Show social proof near price | "Most users upgrade after their first reading" |

---

## 6. AI Summary Copy Rules

When generating AI-powered readings and summaries:

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Use hedged language | "Your chart structure suggests…", "The pattern leans toward…" |
| Reference the method | "Based on the BaZi day-master analysis…" |
| Limit superlatives | Avoid "amazing", "incredible", "life-changing" |
| Offer reflection, not orders | ✅ "Consider whether…" · ❌ "You must…" |
| End with an empowering note | "Use this as a lens, not a verdict." |

---

## 7. Disclaimer Rules

Every page and report that presents interpretive content **must** include a responsible-use notice:

### Required elements

1. **Method attribution** — name the calculation engine (Swiss Ephemeris, classical algorithm, etc.)
2. **Non-replacement clause** — state that readings are for self-reflection, not a substitute for professional advice
3. **Privacy assurance** — mention encryption and no third-party data sharing
4. **Tone** — warm and brief, not legalese

### Template (EN)

> TianJi's analysis is built on rigorous astronomical calculations and classical systems, designed as a tool for self-exploration — not a replacement for professional advice.

### Template (ZH)

> 天机的分析建立在严谨的天文计算和经典命理体系之上，旨在提供有深度的自我探索工具，而非替代专业建议。
