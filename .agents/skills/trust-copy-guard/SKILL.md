# Trust Copy Guard

## Purpose

Keep mystical product copy persuasive without making unsafe or absolute claims.

## When to use

Use before release, after copy-heavy changes, on landing pages, reports, share cards, pricing, ads, and email copy.

## Inputs

- Page copy
- Report templates
- Share copy
- Pricing and trust sections
- Locale files
- Legal/disclaimer pages

## Actions

1. Search for absolute prediction and advice-risk wording.
2. Flag medical, financial, legal, guaranteed, and deterministic claims.
3. Rewrite toward self-reflection, emotional clarity, relationship insight, and entertainment/personal reflection.
4. Preserve conversion intent and premium value.
5. Keep bilingual copy aligned when applicable.

## Constraints

- Do not make copy bland or generic.
- Do not remove disclaimers, trust signals, pricing, FAQ, or upgrade paths.
- Do not introduce new legal policy without review.

## Definition of Done

- Risky claims are removed or softened.
- Copy still communicates value clearly.
- Locale variants remain aligned.
- Review packet lists before/after examples for sensitive copy.

## Validation

- `npm run audit:copy`
- `npm run typecheck` when source files change.
