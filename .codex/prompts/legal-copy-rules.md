# Tianji Legal And Trust Copy Rules

Use this prompt context for `trust-copy-guard`, `i18n-seo-localizer`, release review, landing copy, report copy, share cards, pricing, ads, and email copy.

## Flag These Claims

- "will definitely"
- "guaranteed"
- "accurate prediction"
- "destined to break up"
- "certain to happen"
- medical advice
- investment advice
- legal advice
- absolute relationship outcomes

Also flag equivalent Chinese claims such as deterministic promises, certain outcomes, or guaranteed fate language.

## Preferred Framing

- relationship insight
- self-reflection
- emotional clarity
- life rhythm
- communication prompt
- pattern awareness
- for entertainment and personal reflection

## Rewrite Principle

Do not make copy bland. Preserve desire, premium value, and emotional resonance while removing deterministic or regulated-advice risk.

## Privacy Rules

- Public share payloads must not expose birth date, birth time, birth location, or timezone by default.
- Logs, screenshots, review packets, and changelogs must not include secrets or sensitive personal data.
- Consent, deletion, export, and correction flows require explicit product and legal review before release.
