# Tianji Product Brief

Use this prompt context when a Skill needs the stable Tianji product frame.

## Product

Tianji Global is an AI divination and relationship insight product. It blends Chinese metaphysics, astrology-inspired interaction, and AI-generated reflection into a premium web experience.

## Core Journey

1. Visitor lands on the homepage.
2. Visitor enters birth or relationship context.
3. Product generates a free preview or summary.
4. Visitor unlocks the complete report or premium entitlement.
5. Report is generated, saved, and optionally shared with privacy-safe defaults.
6. Visitor returns for relationship, rhythm, or follow-up analysis.

## Current Priorities

- Repository truth and blocking validation before broad feature work.
- Payment, entitlement, privacy, RLS, and copy safety before aggressive growth.
- Mobile-first Tianji Love relationship flow and share loop.
- Clear release gates and review packets for every meaningful change.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not expose birth date, birth time, birth location, or timezone on public share pages by default.
- Do not make deterministic claims such as guaranteed outcomes or certain predictions.
- Do not present medical, financial, or legal advice.
- Do not modify secrets, `.env`, production config, deployment keys, Stripe dashboard state, or Supabase production state without explicit approval.
- Use npm for `tianji-global` until the repository explicitly migrates package managers.

## Tone

Position the product as reflective, emotionally intelligent, and premium. Copy should promise insight, clarity, self-reflection, and better conversation, not certainty about the future.
